ADDRESSING SPECIFIC ISSUES

AI systems, as powerful as they are, are only as good as the data they're trained on. This means they can inadvertently reflect biases present in their training material.

While many language models employ filters to minimize bias and harmful outputs, my responsibility goes further. I'm committed to ensuring that the content I produce is not only bias-free but also inclusive and accessible to all.

The privacy of my customers is my top priority. I adhere strictly to my list of approved AI tools, each selected for their robust privacy policies. Customer data must never be input into AI tools or Large Language Models (LLMs) not on this list.

Similarly, I'm vigilant about protecting my own intellectual property. By sticking to these approved tools, I safeguard both my customers' privacy and my intellectual assets, ensuring they're not inadvertently used to train publicly accessible models.

AI systems, for all their benefits, can be vulnerable to cyber threats.

Please ensure you're only using AI tools from my approved list. If you're considering any additional tools for use on company devices, these must be discussed and cleared with my security processes first.

In my use of AI, I steer clear of any content that could mislead or manipulate my customers.

All AI-generated content is aligned with my brand values and undergoes a thorough review process. This helps me check for any bias, inaccuracies, or other potential risks, ensuring my content remains ethical and trustworthy.

I uphold a firm policy against using AI to impersonate any individual without their explicit consent. My ethical stance extends to how I handle content inspired by public figures.

While AI offers the ability to create content "in the style" of such figures, I exercise caution and transparency. If ever I choose to use this capability, it will be explicitly stated and done with the utmost respect for the individual's persona.
